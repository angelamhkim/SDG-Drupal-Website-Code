Everyone can contribute to making sure the Global Goals are met in the City of Los Angeles.


If you have any questions or suggestions, email us at sdg@lacity.org.
If you have a project in the City of Los Angeles that is advancing any of the SDGs, submit your project to the SDG Activities Index.
If you have data related to the SDGs, submit your data to us at our Open SDG Data Reporting Platform.
