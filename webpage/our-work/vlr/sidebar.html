The SDG Activities Index is a living encyclopedia of people and organizations doing great work to advance the Sustainable Development Goals in Los Angeles.
Here, you can filter through projects and organizations being led by government, private sector, nonprofits, and philanthropy to identify partners or just learn about the amazing work being done by Angelenos every day.  

We want to see your project listed here! Have a look at the criteria below and if you think this fits, please submit your activity. This Index is a tool to more easily find initiatives occurring in our city that advance the SDGs and benefit L.A. residents. 

This includes projects and organizations that:
1) are working across different sectors or with a range of partners;
2) are focused on trying to address challenges facing our city or enable positive outcomes for Angelenos;
3) are contributing to the Sustainable Development Goals, to helping achieve the world we want, or to making Los Angeles a more sustainable and equitable city.

You can read a more detailed version of our criteria here.
